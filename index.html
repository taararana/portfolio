About Me

I'm a product designer passionate about creating intuitive, user-centered experiences that solve complex problems. Currently at BetMGM, I focus on designing engaging sportsbook experiences that empower users to make confident decisions.

My approach combines deep user research, systematic design thinking, and collaborative problem-solving to create products that truly serve user needs while achieving business goals.
